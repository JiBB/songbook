Rock-a-bye Baby

Categories
baby, lullaby
To the tune of
Lillibullero

Rock-a-by baby
On the tree top,
When the wind blows
The cradle will rock.
When the bough breaks,
The cradle will fall,
And down will fall baby
Cradle and all.
