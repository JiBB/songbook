Itsy Bitsy Spider

Categories
spider, rain
Also known as
Incy Wincy Spider

The itsy bitsy spider climbed up the waterspout.
Down came the rain
and washed the spider out.
Out came the sun
and dried up all the rain
and the itsy bitsy spider climbed up the spout again.
