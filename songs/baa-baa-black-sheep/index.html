Baa, Baa, Black Sheep

Categories
farm animal
To the tune of
Ah! vous dirai-je, maman
Source
Tommy Thumb's Pretty Song Book

Baa, baa, black sheep,
Have you any wool?
Yes, sir, yes, sir,
Three bags full;
One for the master,
And one for the dame,
And one for the little boy
Who lives down the lane.
