I'm a Little Teapot

Categories
tea
Also known as
The Teapot Song
Source
George Harold Sanders and Clarence Z. Kelley
Copyright
George Harold Sanders and Clarence Z. Kelley, 1939

I’m a little teapot
Short and stout
Here is my handle (one hand on hip)
Here is my spout (other hand out, wrist and elbow bent)

When I get all steamed up
Hear me shout
“Tip me over
and pour me out!”
