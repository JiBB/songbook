Title: I'm a Little Teapot
Tags: tea
AKA: The Teapot Song
Source: George Harold Sanders and Clarence Z. Kelley
Copyright: George Harold Sanders and Clarence Z. Kelley, 1939

I'm a little teapot
Short and stout
Here is my handle _(one hand on hip)_
Here is my spout _(other hand out, wrist and elbow bent)_

When I get all steamed up
Hear me shout
"Tip me over
and pour me out!"
